Logical Operators

    ! (logical NOT)
    || (logical OR)
    && (logical AND)

Bitwise Operators

    & (bitwise AND)
    | (bitwise OR)
    ^ (bitwise XOR) -> EXCLUSIVE OR OPERATOR
    &^ (bitwise AND NOT)
    << (left shift)
    >> (right shift)

Comparison Operators
    == (equal)
    != (not equal)
    < (less than)
    <= (less than or equal to)
    > (greater than)
    >= (greater than or equal to)
